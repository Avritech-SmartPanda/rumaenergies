Who We Are

Ruma Energies is an independent reseller of bulk petroleum products, specialising in quality cross-industry fuel supply, fuel delivery and fuel management solutions.

Ruma Energies has major focus on oil and gas. The leadership of the company is committed, responsive and reliable. We are flexible to service our clients at the top of our ability driven by the notion of shared value.
